Make Melbourne Great Again

THEAM

Our team is committed to providing reliable advice and recommendations for the planning and improvement of Melbourne's transport infrastructure.


PROJECT

In this project, we have visualised and analysed Melbourne's traffic flow and congestion levels in relation to the spatial distribution of traffic flows and the setting of traffic lights.


VISUALIZATION

The visualizations provided will enable the Melbourne government to make beneficial improvements to the transport infrastructure.


COMPONENTS

In order for you to make use of this visualisation project, we will briefly introduce the various components. Use the navigation bar on the top to see details.

Year Month Day







Looking at a larger time span, we see that traffic volume and congestion in Melbourne dropped significantly compared to previous years due to the prolonged Lockdown during the COVID-19 outbreak.

However, with the lifting of the lockdown and the arrival of Christmas, traffic volumes and congestion levels have increased significantly.

This is a reminder to the government that more measures should be put in place to deal with retaliatory travel to avoid congestion after the widespread administration of the vaccine and the gradual liberalisation of travel restrictions.

Looking at a smaller time span, this is a reminder that the government should do more to address congestion in the morning and evening peaks, as evidenced by the two peaks in the LineChart day and the huge red Buddle in BuddleChart.
